Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out. Decide of the length of the short from the bottom of the t-shirt. Trace a straight horizontal line from side to side of the t-shirt at the chosen measure. Cut out on the line. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the shirt and pin it to create an hem. You can also insert an elastic band inside the hem to make it more adjustable. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand. To create the short legs, lay the tube flat on a table, and be sure it is inside out. Trace a vertical line, with the desired croth measurements, from the middle of the tube. Cut out on the line. For each leg, sew the front with the back of the tube.
