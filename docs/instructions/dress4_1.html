DRESS GENERATION #004

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. elastic band
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the dress flat on a table, inside out..

2. Trace a straight horizontal line, side to side.

3. Trace a second line, parallel to the first one at the desired distance, knowing that this distance represents the coverage of the top.

4. Cut out on the two lines.

5. Measure your bust and cut an elastic band of the same length.

6. Fold up the previously cut parts on 1 cm on the inside of the top, one with the elastic band inside, and pin them to create hems.

7. Sew the hems with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
