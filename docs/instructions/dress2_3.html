DRESS GENERATION #002

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. elastic band
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the dress flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the length of the skirt and trace a straight line at the desired length.

3. Cut out on the traced line.

4. Measure your waist and cut an elastic band of the same length.

5. Fold up the previously cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the skirt, with the elastic band inside and pin it to create an hem.

6. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
