PANTS GENERATION #002

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. ripper
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the pants flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the length of the skirt, trace a straight line at this height.

3. Cut out on the previously traced line.

4. Unstitch the crotch.

5. Pin the ancient front legs together, same for the back legs.

Sew on the pinned sides.

6. Fold up the previously cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the pants and pin it to create an hem.

7. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
