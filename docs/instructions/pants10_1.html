PANTS GENERATION #010

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. sewing machine

1. Unstitch the crotch.

2.Pin the ancient front legs together, same for the back legs.

Sew the previously pinned sides

3. Lay the tube flat on a table, inside out.

Decide of the length of the top, trace two straight lines with this distance between them.

4. Cut out on the traced lines.

5. Fold up the previously cut parts on 1 cm on the inside of the top and pin them to create hems.

Sew the hems with a sewing machine or by hand.

6. To create straps, use the leftover.

Lay it flat on the table.

7. Draw two rectangles the width you desire plus 1cm for the hem (it will be the width of the strap) and calculate the length to match your shoulder measurements.

8. Cut the out the rectangles.

9. Fold them in half across the width and pin them.

Sew them independently and turn them inside out.

10. Sew them to the top as classic straps or crossed...
You can also still adjust the length of the straps and cut the excess if needed.

You are done!
