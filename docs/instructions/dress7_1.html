DRESS GENERATION #007

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. sewing machine

1. Lay the dress flat on a table, inside out.

2. Use a pair of existing socks for reference and draw their contour plus 1 cm, on the dress.

3. Cut on the lines.

4. Pin the two part of the socks together.

5. Sew with a sewing machine or by hand.

6. Fold up the edges of the socks on 1 cm on the inside (you can add an elastic band inside for better support), and pin them to create hems.

7. Sew the hems with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
