T-SHIRT GENERATION #004

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. ripper
7. sewing machine

1. Unstitch the pocket.

2. Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out.

Trace a straight horizontal line from one side to another of the t-shirt, 2cm under the sleeves.

3. Trace a second line, parallel to the first one at the desired distance, knowing that the distance represents the coverage of the top.

4. Cut out on the two lines.

5. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the shirt and pin it to create an hem.

Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

6. Use the leftover to create straps.

Unbutton the rest of the t-shirt, and lay it flat on the table.

7. Draw two rectangles the width you desire plus 1cm for the hem (it will be the width of the strap) and calculate the length to match your shoulder measurements.

8. Cut the out the rectangles.

9. Fold them in half across the width and pin them.

Sew them independently and turn them inside out.

10. Sew them to the top as classic straps or crossed.
You can also still adjust the length of the straps and cut the excess if needed.

You are done!
