T-SHIRT GENERATION #002

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. elastic band
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the length of the skirt, trace a straight line at this height.

CAUTION!! Try to have a button approximately 4cm under the chosen length so it serves to close/open the skirt.

3. Cut out on the previously traced line.

4. Adjust to your size by trying it on and pinning the sides.
Don’t be afraid to make it a bit tight, as you’ll have the buttons to open it and put it on.

5. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the shirt and pin it to create an hem.
You can also insert an elastic band inside the hem to make it more adjustable.

6. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

CAUTION!! Do not sew on the buttoned up part so you can still open the skirt.

You are done!
