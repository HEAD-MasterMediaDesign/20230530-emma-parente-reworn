T-SHIRT GENERATION #009

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. ripper
7. sewing machine

1. Unstitch one sleeve.

Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the covering of the top and trace a straight line at the desired length.
Cut out on the previously traced line.

3. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the shirt and pin it to create an hem.

4. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

CAUTION!! Do not sew on the buttoned up part so you can still open the t-shirt.

5. To create a sleeve, use the leftover.

Unbutton it, and lay it flat on the table.

6. Mesure the circumference of the already existing sleeves at the shoulder base.

7. On the leftover, draw one rectangle, the width should match the previousley calculated circumference and length corresponds to your arm length.

8. Cut the out the rectangle.

9. Fold it in half across the width and pin it.

Sew on the pinned side and then sew it to the t-shirt sleeve.

You are done!
