PANTS GENERATION #004

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. ripper
7. sewing machine

1. Unstitch the crotch.

2. Lay the pants flat on a table, inside out.

3. Pin the ancient front legs together, same for the back legs.

4. Sew the pinned sides with a sewing machine or by hand.

5. To create the front slit, decide of its height and width and draw a triangle that matches your wishes.

Cut out on the lines.

6. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the skirt and pin it to create an hem.

7. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
