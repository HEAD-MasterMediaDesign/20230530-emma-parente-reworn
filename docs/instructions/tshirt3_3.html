T-SHIRT GENERATION #003

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. ripper
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the length of the top and trace a straight line at this height.

Cut out on the previously traced line.

3. Unstitch the sleeves or cut them right on the stitching if you cannot unsew them.

4. Cut out half a circle around the collar, to remove it.
The size of the circle will define the depth of the neckline, adjust as you wish.

5.Fold up the freshly cut parts (bottom of top, collar and sleeves) on 1 cm on the inside of the t-shirt and pin them to create hems.

6. Sew the hems with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
