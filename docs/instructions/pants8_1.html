PANTS GENERATION #008

1. ruler
2. pencil
3. scissors
4. thread
5. pins
6. elastic band
7. sewing machine

1. Lay the pants flat on a table, inside out.

2. Decide of the length of the gaiter, trace a straight line at this height on each leg.

3. Cut out on the freshly traced lines.

4. Measure your calf (if you want the gaiter to sit on this part of your leg) and cut 2 elastic bands of the same length.

5. Fold up the previously cut parts on 1 cm on the inside of each gaiter, with the elastic band inside and pin them to create hems.

6. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.

You are done!
