- ruler
- pencil
- scissors
- thread
- pins
- sewing machine/needle

1. Lay the t-shirt flat on a table, inside out.
2. Decide of the covering of the top and trace a straight line at the desired length.
3. Cut out on the previously traced line.
4. Fold up the freshly cut part on 1 cm on the inside of the shirt and pin it to create an hem.
5. Sew the hem with a sewing machine or by hand.
CAUTION!! Do not sew on the buttoned up part so you can still open the t-shirt.
